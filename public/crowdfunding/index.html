My Talking Teio

Get ready for My Talking Teio, a virtual pet game in the works that's set to take the world by storm! Our team is hard at work bringing you Tokai Teio, the fiery little horse girl from Uma Musume: Pretty Derby, as your very own digital companion. Feed her carrots to fuel her dreams, douse her with a pool of sweet Hachimi, and tap her to hear her shout, "I'm training to be number one!"
